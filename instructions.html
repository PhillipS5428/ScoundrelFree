Game Instructions

Survival Guide

Welcome to the Endless Dungeons. Your goal is simple: navigate the entire deck of encounters without your health dropping to zero.

The Dungeon Deck

  • Monsters (♣ & ♠): Dangerous foes. Their strength is their rank (2-10). Face cards are stronger: J=11, Q=12, K=13. Aces are weak (1).
  • Weapons (♦): Tools for survival. Equip them to fight back.
  • Potions (♥): Restore your health equal to their rank.

Note: The dungeon is stripped of high-value resources. Red face cards (J, Q, K of ♥/♦) are removed from the deck.

Combat & Tactics

  • The Room: You face 4 cards at a time. You must play or defeat at least 3 of them to refill the room.
  • Bare Hands: You can always fight unarmed, but you will take damage equal to the monster's full strength.
  • Equipping Weapons: Select a Diamond to equip it. You can only hold one weapon at a time; equipping a new one discards the old one.

Weapon Mastery

Weapons in Endless Dungeons have a unique Memory mechanic:

  • Victory: When you defeat a monster with a weapon of equal or greater strength, you take no damage. However, your weapon dulls or sharpens to match the strength of the defeated monster.
  • Overexertion (Training): You may attack a monster stronger than your current weapon once per equipped weapon. You take the difference in damage, and your weapon's strength rises to match the monster.
  • Limit: Once a weapon has been Overexerted (trained up), it cannot be used to attack stronger monsters again. It can only slay weaker foes, slowly losing power with every victory.

Strategic Retreat

If a room is too dangerous, you may Flee. All 4 cards are shuffled to the bottom of the deck. You cannot flee twice in a row; you must face at least one encounter (fight, equip, or heal) before fleeing again.
