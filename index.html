How to Play

Game Play

In each room, play three cards to clear the room and advance to the next. The card's effect depends on its suit and value. each card type.

  • The game defaults to a Quick Attack but it will automatically choose Attack + Shield when a new shield is equipped.
  • To attack a monster, choose your preferred attack type and click on the moster you wish to fight.

    • Card Types

    • Monsters (♠ & ♣): Enemies that attack you. Face cards are powerful!
    • Weapons (♦):
      • Swords (Ace/0): Increase your attack damage.
      • Shields (2-10, J, Q, K): Block monster attacks.
    • Potions (♥): Restore your health.

    Combat & Actions

    • Quick Attack: Strike with your sword. You take damage equal to the Monster's strength minus your Sword's strength.
    • Shield Block: Defend with your shield. If your Shield + Sword is stronger than the Monster, you take no damage. The Monster becomes your new Shield.
    • Fleeing: If the room is full (4 cards), you can flee to shuffle the room back into the deck. You cannot flee twice in a row.
